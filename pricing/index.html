Scope

What every tier includes — and what it doesn't.

Same product across every tier. Tier sets capacity, not feature set.

What's included at every tier

  • 27-check verification across all active subs
  • 8-phase processing on every incoming COI
  • Automated chase sequence (4 to 6 attempts per non-compliant sub)
  • AECLogix-run exception layer
  • Compliance dashboard
  • Escalation tooling for your COI clerk (script, log button, follow-up automation)
  • Monthly executive compliance report
  • Setup inside your existing tools — no new SaaS for your team to adopt

What's not included

  • Phone calls to subcontractors who refuse to respond after the full automation sequence — those go to your COI clerk or PM
  • Legal interpretation of contract language between GC and sub
  • Insurance brokerage or policy procurement for the GC or subs
  • Generic business automation outside the COI verification function
