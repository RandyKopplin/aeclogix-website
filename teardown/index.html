What The Teardown Catches

The gaps that turn an AI status into a suggestion.

The ACORD 25 face is not proof of coverage. The actual coverage lives in the endorsements — and that's where the exposure hides on most certs we see.

→
Additional Insured wording that doesn't match the contract
Blanket vs. scheduled, ongoing vs. completed ops, the wrong CG form attached.
→
Missing primary-and-noncontributory endorsement
Contract requires it. Cert says nothing. Your policy responds first.
→
Waiver of subrogation gaps
No CG 24 04 attached, or attached but limited to ongoing operations only.
→
Limits below what your contract requires
Per-occurrence, aggregate, products/completed ops, umbrella stacking errors.
→
Coverage expiring inside the project window
Cert is current today. Policy expires before substantial completion.
→
Cancellation notice language that doesn't bind the carrier
"Endeavor to" notice is not notice. Most certs still carry it.
